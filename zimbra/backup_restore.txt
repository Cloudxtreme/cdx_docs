Backing Up a Single Account:

root@webmail:/# su zimbra
zimbra@webmail:/tmp/testbackuprestore$ zmbackup -f -t /tmp/testbackuprestore/ -a zimbra6_00@cardiodx.com
(-f = full, -t = target, -a = account)

Restoring a Single Account:

root@zimbra6devel:/# su zimbra
zimbra@zimbra6devel:/tmp$ zmrestore -rf -t /tmp/testbackuprestore/ -a zimbra6_00@cardiodx.com
(-rf = restore full most recent backup, not incremental, -t = target, -a = account)
